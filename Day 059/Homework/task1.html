simple omlete recipe

an easy and puick dish , perfect for any meal this classic omlete combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection optionally filled width your choice of cheese vegetables or meats

preparation time

  • total: appoximately 10 minutes
  • preparation: 5 minutes
  • cooking: 5 minutes

    • ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt, to taste
  • papper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • optional fillings: cheese ,diced vegetables , cooked meats,herbs

    • instuctions

    1. beats the eggs: in a bowl the eggs width a pinch of salt nad papper they are well mixed. you can add tablespoon of water or milk for fluffier texture
    2. heat the pan: place a non stivk frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil
    3. cook the omelette:once the butter is melted and bubbling pour in the eggs tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface
    4. add fillings(optional): when the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle sprinkle your chosen fillings over half of the omelette
    5. fold and serve:as the omelette continues to cook carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings let it cook for another minute then slide it into a plate
    6. Enjoy: serve hot wudth additional salt an pepper if needed
